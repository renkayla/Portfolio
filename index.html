Moody the 5 min Mental Health App

In the hustle and bustle of our daily lives, it's easy to overlook the importance of self-reflection and gratitude. That's why we created Moody, a unique online platform designed to make journaling quick, convenient, and transformative. Moody is not your typical journaling app. We understand that your time is valuable, so we've crafted an experience that allows you to capture your thoughts, emotions, and reflections in just five minutes a day. Whether you're a seasoned journaler or new to the practice, Moody is here to guide you on a journey of self-discovery and personal growth. Our application meets MVP criteria using Node and Express to create a RESTful API, Handlebars as the templating engine, MySQL and Sequelize for the database, and dotenv to protect sensitive information. When logging into Moody, the user sees a randomly generated inspirational quote. This was done using a third-party API. We also used materialize as a CSS framework to make front-end development easier.
